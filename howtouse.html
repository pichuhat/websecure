How to use websecure

Most of us post links all the time. Therefore, some people will click these links. However, some won't. They might think it is a scam or a rickroll.
By using this extremely simple process, you can learn to use websecure for all your favorite websites.

First, go to the spot where you will post the link.
Next, replace the link you are posting with the following:
https://websecure.glitch.me/verify.html?[URL of link here]

Important!

the ? is mandatory in the url, and so is putting https:// at the start. Otherwise the system won't work.
